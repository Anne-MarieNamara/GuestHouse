Our Location

The B&B is located in the heart of Galway city on College Road, with views out over the bay. It is 80km / 50 miles from Shannon Airport and a 2 and a half hour drive from Dubin Airport. The nearest bus stop is only 55 metres/ 60 yards away. - 1 minute walk. Taxi's are available on request.
