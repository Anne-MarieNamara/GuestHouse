Local Attractions

Inismara B&B is within walking distance of the famous Eyre Square and shopping centre, a variety of restaurants and many friendly Irish music pubs. Also available in the area are activites such as horse riding, golfing, fishing, and water sports.

There are two local sandy beaches perfect for strolling and viewing the spectacular coastal walks - Ballyloughnane Beach, an 8 minute drive away, and Ladies Beach, a 10 minute drive away in Salthill.
