Our Rooms

All of our rooms are luxuriously furnished with walnut wooden floors, private bothrooms with full sizes baths and separate walk-in tropical rain showers.

  • Each room provides:
  • Hair dryers
  • Iron and ironing board
  • 32" to 40" flatscreen TV
  • Central heating / air conditioning
  • Tea/Coffee making facilities

The Wild Atlantic Way Suite

Our Wild Atlantic suite will suit any single traveller. It has it's own private balcony and a tropical shower. Feel at home with our comfy tuft and needle mattress.
Price per night: €40

The Moher Suite

Dream away in our Moher suite which can be a Double or Twin Room. It sleeps 2 people max and has lovely views of Galway Bay.
Price per night: €50


The West Suite

Our Triple Room has three single beds with tuft and needle mattresses. This room can also have one double bed and 1 single bed. It sleeps a max of 3 people.
Price per night: €55

The Connacht Suite

This is our Family Room which sleeps two adults and two children. It has 1 double bed with our dream foam mattress and 2 single beds with tuft and needle mattresses. Cots are available on request.
Price per night: €85
