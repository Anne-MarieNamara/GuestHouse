Welcome to Inismara B&B


Inismara is a family run B&B situated in the centre of Galway city, offering spectacular views over Galway Bay. Gillian Moore has been helping visiors relax and see the area for over twenty years.


Let Inismara be your home away from home while you enjoy your stay in Galway. Our guesthouse was purpose built for comfort and we pride ourselves on our ability to go above and beyond to ensure that you have everything you need.Inishmara is just 5 minutes walk from the city centre.

Galway city has many attractions including the famous Eyre Square and shopping centre, the Connaught rugby stadium, and award winning restaurants including The Huntsman . Embrace the culture and vibes of the city from the Irish Traditional buskers on Shop St to the live Trad sessions in the pubs. Whether you're planning a weekend get-away or a romantic holiday, Galway has something for everyone.
